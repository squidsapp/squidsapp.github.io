The Complete
Squids Guide

Everything you need to know to get the most out of Squids — from signing up to going viral on Ocean.

🚀 Getting Started
1

Sign Up

Go to app.squids.co.za. Tap Sign Up. Enter your email address, choose a unique username (3–20 characters: letters, numbers, underscores), enter your date of birth, and set a strong password (8+ chars, 1 uppercase, 1 number). Check your email for a verification code.

Tip: Choose a username you'll be happy with — it becomes your @handle and profile URL. You can change it later in Settings.
2

Onboarding

After verifying your email you'll complete a quick onboarding: upload a profile photo, write a bio (up to 160 characters), choose your interests, and follow your first accounts. Your language is detected automatically.

3

Two-Factor Authentication

Squids automatically protects your account with 2FA. When signing in from a new device or location, a one-time code is sent to your email. You don't need to do anything to set this up — it activates automatically.

4

Guest Mode

Don't want to sign up yet? Tap Continue as Guest on the login page. You can browse public posts and explore the app without creating an account. You'll need an account to post, comment, or follow.

✍️ Creating & Posting
1

Create a Post

Tap the + button in the bottom navigation (mobile) or left sidebar (desktop). The compose box appears. You can write text, attach images/videos/audio, or create a poll.

2

Hashtags & Mentions

Type #word to add a hashtag — your post becomes discoverable in Explore. Type @username to mention someone — they'll get a notification. Both are clickable in the published post.

3

View Post Analytics

On any of your posts, tap the ⋯ menu → View Analytics. See total views (even from people who don't interact), likes, comments, saves, and shares.

4

Save & Bookmark

Tap the 🔖 bookmark icon on any post to save it to your collection. Access saves from your Profile page under the Saved tab.

🌊 Ocean Mode
1

What is Ocean?

Ocean is Squids' fullscreen vertical video feed. Think TikTok — but African. Access it by tapping the 🌊 icon in the navigation. Videos play automatically as you scroll.

2

Controls

Swipe up / scroll down to next video. Tap the screen to pause/play. The sidebar has Like (❤️), Comment (💬), Save (🔖), Share (↗), and Mute (🔇) buttons.

3

Post to Ocean

Any video you post from the Create page automatically appears in Ocean. Portrait videos (2:3) look best on mobile. Landscape (16:9) is shown on desktop.

Pro tip: Post consistently. Videos with more engagement get shown to more people across the continent.
👥 Groups & Communities
1

Discover Groups

Go to the Groups tab. Browse featured communities, search by name or topic, or see suggestions based on your interests.

2

Create a Group

Tap Create Group. Add a name (must be unique), description, and upload a group avatar. Choose Public (anyone can join) or Private (admin approves requests).

3

Admin Tools

As owner/admin: invite members by @username, remove members, promote to admin, and manage join requests. You can also change the group name and avatar at any time.

🔒 Privacy & Security
1

Privacy Settings

Go to Settings → Privacy. Toggle: private followers (only shows 10 to others), hide liked posts, restrict messages from strangers.

2

Report Content

Tap ⋯ on any post or profile → Report. Select a reason. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours. Your report is anonymous.

3

POPIA Compliance

Squids is fully compliant with South Africa's POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act). Your data is processed lawfully, stored securely, and never sold. Read our Privacy Policy.
