What's New

We ship updates constantly. Here's what's been added, improved, and fixed on Squids.

Always improving
🚀
v3.0 March 2026 Major Release
Ocean Mode — Africa's Video Feed
  • Launched Ocean Mode — fullscreen vertical video feed across the platform
  • 2:3 portrait ratio on mobile, 16:9 landscape on desktop
  • Auto-play, loop, and smooth swipe navigation
  • Sidebar controls: Like, Comment, Save, Share, Mute
  • All video posts automatically appear in Ocean feed
  • Engagement-based distribution algorithm launched
📊
v2.8 February 2026 New Feature
Post Analytics for Everyone
  • Every user now has access to detailed post analytics
  • View counts tracked automatically — even from non-followers
  • Likes, comments, saves, and shares broken down per post
  • Accessible via ⋯ → View Analytics on any of your posts
👥
v2.6 January 2026 New Feature
Groups & Communities
  • Create and join public or private groups
  • Full admin tools: invite, remove, promote members
  • Private group join requests and approval flow
  • Custom group avatars and descriptions
  • Group-specific feed separate from home feed
🌍
v2.5 December 2025 New Feature
Multilingual Support
  • Full support for 5 languages: English, French, Arabic, Swahili, Amharic
  • Automatic language detection based on your location
  • Full right-to-left (RTL) layout for Arabic
  • Language preference saved to your account
🛡️
v2.3 November 2025 Security
Automatic 2FA & IP Protection
  • Automatic Two-Factor Authentication activated on new device logins
  • IP ban system for suspicious or abusive activity
  • Trusted device memory — skip 2FA on known devices
  • POPIA compliance documentation and privacy policy updated
🔭
v2.0 October 2025 Major Release
Explore, Profile Cards & DMs
  • Launched Explore page with hashtag search, people discovery, and trending topics
  • Direct Messages with real-time delivery and emoji reactions
  • Downloadable profile card — share your Squids profile as an image
  • Notification preferences — toggle likes, follows, comments, DMs
  • Privacy controls: hide followers list, liked posts visibility
🦑
v1.0 2024 Launch
Squids Launched
  • Social feed with text, image, video, audio, and poll posts
  • User profiles with avatar, bio, and follower system
  • Hashtags and @mentions throughout the platform
  • Sign up, login, onboarding, and email verification
  • Guest mode for browsing without an account
  • Mobile-responsive design for any device
