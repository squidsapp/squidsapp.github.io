HOW IT WORKS

Your complete guide to Squids

01

Sign Up or Log In

Go to app.squids.co.za and tap Sign Up. Enter your email, choose a username (3–20 characters), and set a secure password. You'll receive an email verification code.

02

Complete Onboarding

Upload your avatar, write a short bio, choose your interests, and follow some accounts to get your feed started. Your language is auto-detected based on your location.

03

Explore & Follow

Head to the Explore page to discover trending hashtags and people to follow. Your home feed shows posts from everyone you follow, updated in real time.

04

Enable Two-Factor Auth

Squids automatically activates 2FA when you sign in from an unknown device. A one-time code is sent to your email. No setup required.

01

Create a Post

Tap the + button. Share text, images, videos, audio, or a poll. Combine multiple types in one post.

02

Use Hashtags & Mentions

Type #hashtag to tag topics. Use @username to mention and notify another user. Both are clickable and searchable.

03

Post Analytics

Tap ⋯ → View Analytics to see who viewed, liked, and saved your post. View counts are tracked automatically.

04

Save Posts

Tap the 🔖 bookmark icon on any post to save it. Access saved posts from your Profile under Saved.

01

What is Ocean Mode?

Ocean 🌊 is Squids' vertical video feed. Fullscreen, swipe-up experience. 2:3 portrait on mobile, 16:9 on desktop.

02

Navigate Ocean

Swipe up or scroll to move to the next video. Tap anywhere to pause/play. The right sidebar has Like, Comment, Save, Share, and Mute.

03

Get Featured in Ocean

Post any video from the Create page. It automatically appears in Ocean, sorted by newest. More engagement = wider reach.

01

Join or Create a Group

Go to Groups. Browse communities and tap Join. For private groups, send a request. Create your own group with a name, description, and avatar.

02

Group Admin Tools

Invite members, remove members, promote to admin, and accept/decline join requests for private groups.

03

Post in Groups

Posts appear in both the group feed and your profile. Only members can post in private groups.

01

Edit Your Profile

Tap Edit Profile. Update your display name, username, bio (160 chars), website, and location.

02

Upload Avatar & Cover

Tap the 📷 camera icon on your avatar or banner to upload new images.

03

Download Profile Card

Tap the ⬇️ icon to generate a shareable profile card. Share it on WhatsApp, X, and more.

01

Privacy Settings

Go to Settings → Privacy. Control who sees your followers, liked posts, and whether strangers can message you.

02

Two-Factor Auth

Squids auto-activates 2FA on unknown devices. A code is sent to your email — no setup needed.

03

Block & Report

Tap ⋯ → Report to flag harmful content. Our team reviews all reports within 24 hours.

04

POPIA Compliance

Your data is processed lawfully, stored securely, never sold. See our Privacy Policy.
