HOW IT WORKS

Your complete guide to Squids

01

Sign Up or Log In

Go to app.squids.co.za and tap Sign Up. Enter your email, choose a username (3–20 characters, letters/numbers/underscores), and set a secure password. You'll receive an email verification code to confirm your account.

02

Complete Onboarding

After signing up, you'll be guided through a quick onboarding flow. Upload your avatar, write a short bio, choose your interests, and follow some accounts to get your feed started. Your language is auto-detected based on your location.

03

Explore & Follow

Head to the Explore page to discover trending hashtags and people to follow. Search by username or topic. Your home feed shows posts from everyone you follow, updated in real time.

04

Enable Two-Factor Auth

Squids automatically activates 2FA when you sign in from an unknown device. A one-time code is sent to your email. This keeps your account protected without any setup required.

01

Create a Post

Tap the + button (bottom centre on mobile, left sidebar on desktop). You can share text, upload images, videos, audio files, or create a poll. Combine multiple types in one post.

02

Use Hashtags & Mentions

Type #hashtag to tag topics and make your post discoverable in Explore. Use @username to mention and notify another user. Both are clickable and searchable across the app.

03

Post Analytics

Tap the 📊 icon on your post or tap ⋯ → View Analytics to see who viewed, liked, and saved your post. View counts are tracked automatically even if someone doesn't engage.

04

Save Posts

Tap the 🔖 bookmark icon on any post to save it. Access all saved posts from your Profile tab under Saved. You can also tap ⋯ → Save post from the menu.

01

What is Ocean Mode?

Ocean 🌊 is Squids' vertical video feed — similar to TikTok. It shows video posts from across the platform in a fullscreen, swipe-up experience. On mobile it's 2:3 portrait, on desktop it's 16:9 landscape.

02

Navigate Ocean

Swipe up or scroll to move to the next video. Each video auto-plays and loops. Tap anywhere to pause/play. The right sidebar has Like, Comment, Save, Share, and Mute controls.

03

Get Featured in Ocean

Post a video from the Create page. Any video post automatically appears in Ocean, sorted by newest first. The more engagement your video gets, the wider it spreads across feeds.

01

Join or Create a Group

Go to Groups from the sidebar or navigation. Browse existing communities and tap Join. For private groups, send a join request that the admin must approve. Create your own group with a name, description, and avatar.

02

Group Admin Tools

As a group owner or admin you can: upload a group avatar, invite members by username, remove members, promote members to admin, and accept or decline join requests for private groups.

03

Post in Groups

Once you're a member, the composer appears inside the group. Posts made in a group appear in both the group feed and your profile. Only members can post in private groups.

01

Edit Your Profile

Go to your profile and tap Edit Profile. Update your display name, username, bio (up to 160 characters), website, and location. Changing your username updates your profile URL automatically.

02

Upload Avatar & Cover

Tap the 📷 camera icon on your avatar or banner to upload new images. Avatars display as circles throughout the app. Banners appear at the top of your profile page.

03

Followers & Following

Tap the Followers or Following count on any profile to see the full list. If a profile is private, only 10 are shown to others — but the account owner can always see all.

04

Download Your Profile Card

Tap the ⬇️ download icon on your profile to generate a shareable profile card image. Share it on WhatsApp, other platforms, or anywhere else to promote your Squids page.

01

Privacy Settings

Go to Settings → Privacy to control: who can see your followers list (private mode shows only 10 to others), whether your liked posts are visible, and whether strangers can message you.

02

Notification Controls

Under Settings → Notifications, toggle on or off: likes, replies/comments, new followers, and direct messages. Changes save immediately to your account.

03

Block & Report

Tap ⋯ on any post and choose Report to flag harmful content. Our team reviews all reports. Reported content is reviewed within 24 hours. Your safety is our priority.

04

POPIA Compliance

Squids complies with South Africa's POPIA (Protection of Personal Information Act). Your data is processed lawfully, stored securely, and never sold to third parties. See our Privacy Policy at policies.squids.co.za/privacy.

THE STORY

Built in Africa, for Africa

Squids was created by Vuyani Siyanda Vilakazi, a South African developer and founder of TyVila.Online. The vision was simple: build a social platform that truly understands Africa — its languages, its communities, its creators, and its culture.

Most global social platforms treat Africa as an afterthought. Squids puts Africa first. From automatic language detection based on your location, to Ocean mode built for mobile-first users, to POPIA compliance for South African privacy laws — every decision was made with African users in mind.

TyVila.Online is the technology company behind Squids, building digital tools that empower Africans online. Squids is their flagship product and the beginning of a larger ecosystem.
