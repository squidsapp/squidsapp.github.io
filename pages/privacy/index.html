Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 March 2026

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account (username, email, profile details), content you post, and usage data to improve the platform. We never collect more than necessary.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve Squids, send you notifications you've opted into, and keep the platform safe. We do not use your data for advertising profiling or sell it to third parties.

Data Protection

Squids is POPIA compliant. Your messages are end-to-end encrypted. We store data securely using industry-standard encryption. You can request a copy or deletion of your data at any time.

Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We use essential third-party services (authentication, hosting) that are contractually required to protect your data.

Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to access your data, correct inaccuracies, request deletion, and object to processing. Contact support@squids.co.za to exercise these rights.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies required for the platform to function (session management, security). No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

Contact

For privacy enquiries, contact us at support@squids.co.za. We respond within 5 business days.
