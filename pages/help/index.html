Help Centre

How do I create an account?

Visit app.squids.co.za and click 'Sign up'. Enter your email and choose a username. It takes under 60 seconds.

How does the Verified Badge work?

The first 200 users to create and complete their Squids profile receive a permanent Verified Badge automatically. No application needed.

How do I reset my password?

On the login page, click 'Forgot password' and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app, scroll to the bottom, and tap 'Delete account'. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

How do I report someone?

On any post or profile, tap the three-dot menu and select 'Report'. Choose a reason and submit. Our team reviews reports within 24-48 hours.

How does Ocean (music) work?

Ocean is Squids' built-in music feature. Find it in the main navigation. You can stream tracks, create playlists, and upload your own music.

What languages does Squids support?

Squids supports English, Arabic (with RTL), French, Swahili, Zulu, Portuguese, Spanish, Hausa, and Yoruba. Change your language in Settings.

How do I contact support?

Email support@squids.co.za — we respond within 24 hours, Monday to Saturday.
