About

Built for Africa

Squids is Africa's social platform, created to connect communities across the continent.

Our Mission

Africa is home to over one billion people, 54 countries, and thousands of languages and cultures. Squids exists to connect them — to give every African a platform to share their story, build community, and grow together.

The Platform

Squids offers a social feed, encrypted messaging, Ocean music streaming, community groups, Q&A, and verified profiles. Everything is free. Every account, every feature, every language — free forever.
