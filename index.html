Questions

Frequently asked

How do I get the free Verified Badge?
Be among the first 200 users to create an account on Squids at app.squids.co.za. Your Verified Badge is applied automatically after you complete your profile — no application, no cost, no waiting. It's permanent.
Is Squids really free?
Yes, completely. Creating an account, posting, messaging, joining groups, using Q&A, streaming on Ocean — all core features are free forever. No hidden charges, no credit card required.
Which countries can use Squids?
Squids is open to everyone worldwide. We have a strong focus on Africa — South Africa, Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, Ethiopia, Egypt, Tanzania, Uganda, Zimbabwe, Cameroon, and all 54 African nations — but anyone anywhere can join.
What languages does Squids support?
Squids currently supports English, Arabic, French, Swahili, Zulu, Portuguese, Spanish, Hausa, and Yoruba. More languages are being added regularly. Arabic has full RTL support.
Is my data safe on Squids?
Yes. Squids is POPIA compliant. We never sell your data to advertisers or third parties. Your messages are end-to-end encrypted. You have full control over your privacy settings, who can see your posts, and what data you share.
How do I contact support?
Email us at support@squids.co.za — our support team responds within 24 hours, Monday through Saturday. You can also reach the @squids account directly on the app for platform announcements and updates.
What is Ocean on Squids?
Ocean is Squids' built-in music streaming feature. Independent African artists can upload and share their music, and listeners can discover, stream, and support them directly on the platform without leaving the app.
