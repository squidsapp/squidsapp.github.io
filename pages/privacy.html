Privacy at Squids

The short version — we respect your privacy, always.

🔒 POPIA Compliant · South Africa

🔒 What we collect

  • Email address (for account and 2FA)
  • Username and display name
  • Profile photo and bio (if you upload them)
  • Posts, comments, and interactions you make
  • IP address (for security and ban enforcement only)

🚫 What we never do

  • We never sell your data to advertisers or third parties
  • We don't show ads in your feed
  • We don't share your personal information without consent
  • We don't store sensitive payment information

🛡️ How we protect your data

  • All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Supabase-backed secure storage
  • Automatic 2FA on new device logins
  • IP banning for abusive or malicious activity

⚖️ POPIA Compliance

Squids complies with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA, Act 4 of 2013). Your personal data is processed lawfully, collected for a specific purpose, and kept for no longer than necessary.

📋 Your rights

  • Access your personal data at any time from Settings
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

📬 Contact

Privacy questions? Contact us at support@squids.co.za. For the full legal Privacy Policy, visit policies.squids.co.za/privacy.
